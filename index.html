Model assumptions: Demo datasets are synthetic and designed to simulate supply chain planning scenarios. This demo uses synthetic inventory data and an interpretable heuristic model based on common planning concepts: days of cover, lead time, safety stock, reorder quantity, and supplier reliability. Thresholds are configurable demo assumptions; Optimize Model demonstrates how historical weekly demand can tune those assumptions.
